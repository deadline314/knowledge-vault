## Summary (short description — max 132 characters)
Right‑click to clip any web page or YouTube video — with subtitles — into Google Drive as Markdown, Text, or PDF.

## Detailed description (English)
Squirl turns any web page or YouTube video into a clean, saved file in one click.

WHAT IT DOES
• Clip the page you're viewing to your Google Drive, or download it locally.
• Choose the output format: Markdown, plain Text, or PDF (with embedded CJK fonts).
• For YouTube, grab the subtitles/transcript and fold them right into the file — pick the language from a menu (no typing language codes).
• Optionally sync clips into your own self‑hosted "AI Desktop" knowledge base.

HOW IT WORKS
• Right‑click anywhere and choose "Clip to Drive", or open the toolbar popup.
• Squirl reads only the page you choose to clip — nothing runs in the background.
• Files land in the Drive folder you pick, named after the page title.

PRIVACY
• Your clips go to YOUR Google Drive and/or YOUR computer. Squirl has no server of its own.
• Settings are stored locally in your browser. No analytics, no tracking, no selling data.

Squirl is available in 8 interface languages: English, 繁體中文, 简体中文, 日本語, 한국어, Español, Русский, Português (BR).

## 詳細說明（繁體中文）
Squirl 讓你一鍵把任何網頁或 YouTube 影片存成乾淨的檔案。

功能
• 把目前瀏覽的頁面剪存到你的 Google Drive，或直接下載到本機。
• 可選輸出格式：Markdown、純文字、PDF（內嵌中文字型）。
• YouTube 影片可一併抓字幕／逐字稿並融入檔案——語言用選單挑選，不必手打語言代碼。
• 可選擇把剪存同步到你自架的「AI Desktop」知識庫。

使用方式
• 在頁面上按右鍵選「剪存到雲端硬碟」，或開工具列的彈出視窗。
• Squirl 只讀取你主動剪存的那個頁面，背景不會擷取任何資料。

隱私
• 檔案存到「你的」Google Drive 或「你的」電腦；Squirl 沒有自己的伺服器。
• 設定只存在你的瀏覽器本機。無分析、無追蹤、不販售資料。

介面支援 8 種語言：English、繁體中文、简体中文、日本語、한국어、Español、Русский、Português (BR)。
